# 📘 PROJECT REPORT

## **NeuroPlan AI – Adaptive Study Intelligence System**

---

## **1. Abstract**

In today’s fast-paced academic environment, students often face challenges in managing their time, prioritizing subjects, and maintaining consistent study habits. Traditional study planners fail to adapt to individual needs and behavioral patterns.

This project, *NeuroPlan AI*, presents an intelligent, adaptive study planning system that generates personalized schedules based on subject difficulty, exam proximity, and preparation level. The system incorporates AI-inspired logic, behavioral analysis, and data visualization to provide an efficient and user-friendly solution for academic planning.

---

## **2. Introduction**

Time management and structured study planning are critical factors for academic success. However, most students rely on static timetables that do not adapt to changing priorities or performance levels.

NeuroPlan AI is designed to overcome these limitations by acting as a **dynamic and intelligent study assistant**. It continuously analyzes user inputs and generates optimized study plans, helping students focus on weak areas while maintaining balance to avoid burnout.

---

## **3. Problem Statement**

Students commonly experience:

* Lack of effective study planning
* Difficulty in prioritizing subjects
* Procrastination and inconsistency
* Overloading or underutilization of study time
* Absence of personalized guidance

Existing tools are static and do not provide **adaptive or intelligent recommendations**.

---

## **4. Objectives**

The main objectives of this project are:

* To develop an intelligent study planning system
* To provide personalized study recommendations
* To incorporate adaptive and behavior-based logic
* To improve student productivity and consistency
* To create a visually interactive and user-friendly interface

## **5. Methodology**

The system is designed using the following approaches:

### **5.1 Rule-Based Decision System**

The study plan is generated using predefined rules based on:

* Subject difficulty
* Exam deadlines
* Preparation percentage

---

### **5.2 Adaptive Logic**

The system dynamically adjusts priorities by analyzing:

* Low preparation levels
* Urgent exam schedules

---

### **5.3 Heuristic Optimization**

Heuristics are used to:

* Balance study workload
* Allocate time efficiently
* Prevent cognitive overload

---

### **5.4 Behavioral Analysis**

The system identifies:

* Weak subjects
* Productivity patterns

---

### **5.5 Data Visualization**

Charts and analytics are used to present:

* Study distribution
* Progress insights
